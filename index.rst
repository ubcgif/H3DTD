H3DTD v2 Package
================

H3DTD v2 is a program library for carrying out forward modeling and inversion of time domain EM data over 3D structures. It is capable of modeling the Cartesian components of the EM fields (E, B and dB/dt) or sub-audio magnetic (SAM) data. The contents of this manual are as follows:
